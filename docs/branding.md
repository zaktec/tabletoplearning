# Branding Guide

## Brand Summary

Tabletoplearning is a warm, modern, and education-focused brand that combines approachable design with trusted learning support.

## Brand Personality

- Friendly
- Encouraging
- Professional
- Practical
- Modern

## Core Brand Colors

- Primary accent: #d97706
- Secondary accent: #f59e0b
- Background cream: #fffaf2
- Soft background: #fff8ec
- Surface card: #fffdfa
- Primary text: #2f241d
- Secondary text: #5d4b3d

## Typography

- Headings: Poppins, bold and modern
- Body text: Inter, clean and readable
- Navigation: Inter, medium weight

## Visual Style

- Soft warm gradients
- Rounded cards
- Spacious layout
- Clear calls to action
- Calm, premium feel

## Logo Direction

- Use a simple text-based wordmark
- Keep the look clean and readable
- Avoid overly complex symbols

## Messaging Tone

Use encouraging, clear, and student-focused language.
Examples:

- Learn with confidence
- Guided support that feels personal
- Build skills step by step
